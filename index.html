Welcome to a Kalman filter simulation demo.

Continuous time kinematic equations for height of falling object:
Velocity: $\dot{h} = \dot{h}(t-\Delta t) - g \Delta t$
Height (position): $ h(t)= h(t-\Delta t) + \dot{h}(t-\Delta t) - \frac{1}{2} g (\Delta t)^2$

Since we're running a loop, we convert the continuous equation to one described in terms of a discrete time index, $k$, where $t=k\Delta t$:
${\dot{h}}_k = {\dot{h}}_{k-1}-g\Delta t$
$h_k = h_{k-1} + {\dot{h}}_{k-1} \Delta t - \frac{1}{2}g (\Delta t)^2$
